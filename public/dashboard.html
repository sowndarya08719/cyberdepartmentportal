Computer Science and Engineering (Cyber Security)

Overview

The department was established with an undergraduate programme in Computer Science and Engineering (Cyber Security) in 2020. This specialization branch provides students technical knowledge in Cyber Security, Digital Forensics and emerging areas such as Device Security and Cyber Forensics.

The department is equipped with modern laboratories such as Cryptography Lab, Network Security Lab, Internet and Web Application Security Lab, Ethical Hacking Lab, Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing Lab, Malware Analysis Lab, Secure Coding Lab and Digital Forensics Lab.

Vision

To develop technically skilled cyber security professionals by imparting innovative knowledge and providing an environment that addresses modern cyber security challenges.

Mission

  • To educate and train students in Cyber Security and Cyber Forensics.
  • To establish a Centre of Research in Cyber Security.
  • To enhance entrepreneurship skills through industry collaboration.

Programme Educational Objectives (PEOs)

PEO1

Graduates will apply knowledge of cyber security and computing to solve real world problems.

PEO2

Graduates will pursue higher education and research in cyber security and related areas.

PEO3

Graduates will demonstrate professional ethics, teamwork and communication skills.

Programme Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

PSO1

Analyze cyber threats and design secure systems using cyber security tools.

PSO2

Apply ethical hacking and digital forensics techniques to identify vulnerabilities.
